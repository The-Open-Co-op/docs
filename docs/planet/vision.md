# Vision

PLANET is a Co-Operating System — digital commons infrastructure owned and shaped by the people who use it.

Every member can contribute to making it better.

We believe digital tools should support your wellbeing, not exploit your attention. PLANET connects you to trusted people, helps you organise your life, and supports the communities you care about — then gets out of your way. The whole point is to get you offline as quickly as possible, back to the people and places that matter.

This is infrastructure for the long term. Not a startup looking for an exit. Not a platform extracting value from your attention. Commons infrastructure — built collaboratively, held in trust, designed to outlast any of us.

The apps and tools that run on PLANET are built by its community, funded by its members, and available to everyone. When you improve PLANET, you improve it for all of us. When you contribute — code, ideas, time, money, or simply by inviting someone you trust — you're helping build shared infrastructure for a regenerative economy.

That economy starts with trust between real people. It grows through communities that govern themselves. And it leads, ultimately, to a fully decentralised system where people and planet come before profit.

We're not building another platform. We're building the foundation for the next era of the internet.
